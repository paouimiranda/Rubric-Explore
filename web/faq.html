Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Rubric — notes, quizzes, account data, and privacy.

What is Rubric?

Rubric is a study companion app that helps learners create and organize notes, build custom quizzes, and monitor study progress. It's designed for active review and quick retrieval.

How do I create, edit, or delete a note?

Create: Go to the Notes screen and tap the “+” button, enter your content, then Save.
Edit: Open any note → tap the edit icon → Save changes.
Delete: Tap Delete inside the note to remove it permanently from your account.

How do quizzes work?

Create quizzes from topics or notes. After creating a quiz you can take it, view results (score/time), and re-run it. Quiz attempts are saved to help you track learning progress.

Does Rubric work offline?

Some features work offline — viewing previously loaded notes and taking cached quizzes. Creating new notes or syncing quiz results requires an internet connection to save data to your account.

Will I receive notifications and reminders?

Yes — Rubric can send study reminders and quiz notifications. Notifications are delivered only if you enable them on your device and in the app settings.

Is my data secure and private?

Your information is stored securely with encrypted transport and authenticated access. Sensitive data like passwords is hashed. We do not share personal data with unauthorized third parties.

What data does Rubric collect and store?

We store notes and quiz definitions, quiz attempts and timestamps, app settings, and account info (email/username). We may retain anonymized logs for security and debugging with short retention periods.

How do I request support or delete my account?

Support: Email rubric.capstone@gmail.com and include a short description of your issue.
Delete account: Send an email from your registered address with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Account deletion is permanent; active data is removed within 30 days and backups/logs may take up to 90 days to purge.
