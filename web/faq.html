Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rubric?
Rubric is a study companion app that helps learners create and organize notes, build custom quizzes, and monitor study progress.
How do I create, edit, or delete a note?
Create: Go to Notes → “+” → Save.
Edit: Open a note → Edit icon → Save.
Delete: Tap Delete to remove permanently.
How do quizzes work?
Create quizzes from topics or notes. You can take them, view results, and re-run them. Attempts are saved for tracking progress.
Is my data secure and private?
All data is transmitted securely and stored with authentication.
What data does Rubric collect?
Notes, quiz definitions and attempts, logs for the main features of the application and account info (email/username).
How do I request support or delete my account?
Support: Email rubric.capstone@gmail.com.
Delete account: Send an email from your registered address with subject “Account Deletion Request”. Data is purged within 30–90 days.
